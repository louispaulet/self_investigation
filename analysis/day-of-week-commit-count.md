# Day-of-Week Commit Count

Using the 506 commits collected over the last 3 months, the commit counts by day of week are:

- Sunday: 142
- Friday: 108
- Saturday: 101
- Monday: 74
- Thursday: 42
- Wednesday: 31
- Tuesday: 8

## Observations

- Sunday is clearly the most active day.
- Friday and Saturday are also very strong commit days.
- Tuesday is by far the quietest day.
- The pattern suggests a lot of work happens on weekends, especially late in the week and on Sunday.

## Interpretation

This looks like a very weekend-heavy workflow, with a strong concentration of activity from Friday through Sunday. Midweek activity is much lower, especially on Tuesday.
